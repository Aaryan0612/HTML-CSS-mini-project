Frequently Asked

How do I pair my NEXUS watch?

Turn on Bluetooth on your phone and open the NEXUS app. Hold the side button on your watch for 3 seconds until the logo appears.

Is the NEXUS Sport waterproof?

Yes, all Series 7 watches are rated WR50, meaning they are water-resistant up to 50 meters and suitable for swimming.

What is the battery life?

The NEXUS Sport lasts up to 48 hours on a single charge with normal use. GPS tracking may reduce this to 18 hours.
